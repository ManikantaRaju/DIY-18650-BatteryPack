Make your own battery pack that's automotive qualified

Ever wanted to make your own battery pack? Well, you can!! This repo is a complete guide to start making your own battery pack from scratch. You will find circuit designs, battery pack designs, thermal systems, Battery Management systems and lot more. Does it sound too much? What if i say the battery packs we make would be modular and can fit different purposes? Pumped up right! I am too a curious DIY-enthusiast like you.

Repo Highlights

  • Modular Battery Pack
  • Battery Management Systems for any battery chemistry (One size that fits all)
  • Thermal Management systems
  • Electronics Control Design using Widely available Open Source hardware like Arduino, Raspberry-Pi, Tiva Boards, etc
